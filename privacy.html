Privacy Policy for Tivvity

Effective Date: February 2026

Tivvity (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Tivvity collects a User ID to enable account functionality such as authentication, saving data, and syncing across devices.

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, or audio
  • Advertising or tracking data

How We Use Information

User IDs are used solely for:

  • App functionality
  • Account management
  • Secure access to features

We do not use data for advertising or tracking.

Third-Party Services

Tivvity uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication and data storage
  • RevenueCat — for subscription and purchase management
  • Apple In-App Purchases — for processing payments

These services may collect limited data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide app functionality.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting kirkowenscodes@gmail.comCopied!.

Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

Tivvity offers auto-renewable subscriptions. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. No personal financial information is collected or stored by Tivvity.

Children’s Privacy

Tivvity does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
